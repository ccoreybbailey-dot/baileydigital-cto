# Thesis & Portfolio

## The bet

BaileyDigital builds focused, AI-powered digital products for specific trades
industries. The moat is insider knowledge: Corey works the trades full-time
(landscaping + construction, Eastern Townships, Quebec) and builds for problems he's
lived, not problems he's guessed at. Generic SaaS companies can't see these problems;
trades people can't build the software. That intersection is the whole advantage.

**Business model:** SaaS products at the core, with room for custom builds and
services alongside. Each product targets a narrow trade vertical and earns trust
through real relationships and field-tested usefulness — not ads, not scale-first.

## The goal

Replace trades income with BaileyDigital revenue. Everything sequences toward that —
not vanity metrics, not feature count. **Paying customers first.**

## Who's building it

Corey, as product owner. He makes the design, product, and prioritization calls,
tests in the field, and uses Claude Code for technical execution. No formal software
background — relies on plain-English explanations and copy-pasteable steps. Background
in filmmaking and storytelling, which feeds the brand and go-to-market. Personal
brand: "a tradesperson building software for the trades" (Indie Hackers, LinkedIn),
with dog + motorcycle lifestyle content as connective tissue. Learns by building, not
by tutorials.

## Portfolio

**ChantierLog — live, primary.**
Daily job-site reporting for trades companies. Crew leads file daily sheets from the
field; admins get an automated Monday-morning email summary with zero login required
(the primary selling feature). Built and piloting with Bessette (Corey's employer,
first customer). Strategic role: this is the proof-of-concept and calling card for
BaileyDigital — a narrow niche with a low revenue ceiling, valuable mainly for the
relationships, credibility, and reusable patterns it generates, **not** as a
standalone scalable business.

**Focal — parked.**
Personal productivity/focus app (React + Vite + Tailwind, localStorage).
Deprioritized in favor of full focus on ChantierLog.

**Email-agent — parked idea.**
Admin manages the backend by replying to plain-language emails that an AI parses into
actions. Flagged as a potentially strong differentiator for future products. Not
started.
